# User Manual: Gajraj Billing Software

## 🚀 Getting Started
Welcome to your new billing system. This guide will help you manage your store efficiently and stay GST compliant.

---

## 1. Billing & Invoicing
1.  **Create Invoice**: Go to the **Billing** tab. Select a customer (or use Walk-in). Add products by searching or scanning.
2.  **GST Options**: The system auto-calculates SGST/CGST or IGST based on the customer's state. You can toggle GST on/off for specific bills.
3.  **Printing**: Click **Print A4 PDF**. The system will generate a professional invoice including:
    - Scannable UPI QR Code for payments.
    - HSN/SAC Summary table.
    - Professional Banker details.

## 2. Inventory Management
1.  **Add Products**: Go to **Products** and click **Create Product**. 
2.  **Settings Alert**: Set a `Low Stock Alert` value (e.g., 10). When stock hits this number, the product will appear in the **Low Stock** dashboard.
3.  **Bulk Download**: Export your entire inventory to Excel anytime.

## 3. Reports & CA Sync
1.  **Sales Reports**: Filter by Date Range in the **Reports** tab.
2.  **Send to CA**: Click the **WhatsApp to CA** button. It will automatically bundle all invoices for that month and open a chat with your Chartered Accountant.

## 4. Safety & Backup
1.  **Secure Deletion**: To delete a customer or product, you must **type their name** exactly as shown. This prevents accidental deletions.
2.  **Daily Backup**: Go to **Settings &rarr; Data Management**. Click **Download .sqlite Backup**. Save this to your Google Drive or a Pen Drive daily.
3.  **App Password**: If email alerts stop working, ensure your "Google App Password" is still active in settings.
